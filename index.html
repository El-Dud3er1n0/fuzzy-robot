What We Do

Ethos

We began Broadhead Design and Build with one focus: to build a construction company that can compete with large firms while operating with the flexibility and customer-focus of a small, family-run business.

We call it small business values with a big business approach.

Location

We operate from 3 bases in South Manchester, which are located in Cheadle, Sale and Heald Green.

Operating in close proximity to our bases, allows us to maximise our resources to ensure your project is completed as quickly as possible, to the highest possible standards.

Experience

Our management team brings solid business experience to our construction business. Their most important job is to bring the best out in our team of experienced employees and contractors, all of whom operate our projects with the care, commitment and customer-focus our reputation is built on.
