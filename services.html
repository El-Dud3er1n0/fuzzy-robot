New Builds

We build many different styles of new build properties, from innovative design-led projects to traditional detached homes. To carry out your new build project, BDB can either provide a full design-build service or act as contractors taking over at the build stage once the plans have been drawn up and planning permission granted.

Building a new home requires good planning and organisation across a number of trades. Over the years we have formed very strong working partnerships with architects, surveyors, structural engineers, sub-contractors and trade specialists enabling us to provide you with a complete construction service supported by everyone involved with your project from start to finish.

Extensions

A house extension can add value to your property and create the valuable extra living space you may need in your home.

We have experience of building all types of extension projects such as side, rear and rooftop extensions. If you are considering extending your home, there are two ways in which we can help you with your project. We can either be involved from the outset by meeting with you on site to go through your requirements, and then contracting or recommending a known architect to draw the initial plans and handle the planning and building regulations application.

Or, if you have already reached this stage then we can simply act as Contractors who take over the project at the build stage. Either way we can offer you guidance and advice throughout the project, giving you peace of mind.

Loft Conversions

We’ve been designing and creating loft conversions in Greater Manchester for over 60 years. To us, every loft conversion is unique; that’s why we’re on hand from the start, to guide you through every detail - from your free feasibility study through to your completed loft.

We’re proud of our discerning approach to design, quality construction and bespoke styling. If you’re planning a loft conversion we’ll have a dedicated team of experts and craftsmen on hand to make sure everything runs smoothly, professionally and on time.

Renovations

We have carried out a variety of refurbishment projects across Dorset and have a wealth of experience in this type of work. This is largely due to the number rural properties in the surrounding area with unused outbuildings or barns which can either be beautifully restored to their original state, or carefully renovated to create a contemporary living space whilst retaining the property’s traditional character.

We can manage your project from start to finish with the care and detailed attention required to achieve high specifications, often carrying out specialist works for those bespoke features which make your home unique. This is only achieved by working closely as a team alongside our carpenters, plumbers, electricians and decorators all under one roof to create your ideal home.
